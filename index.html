SPACATION

Spacation is a new venture dedicated to the exploration of space for the purpose of one day having the opportunity for vacations in space.

Environment friendly, family safe, and super fun are all terms that we use to describe our company. In everything and anything we set our minds to do, we will achieve it as a group. We will think together, but as individuals. We will work hard, but also make time for family and relaxation. We will dream impossible dreams and then design ways to achieve them. We will make Spacation a reality where people can dream big and vacation in the great abyss that is known as "Space."
