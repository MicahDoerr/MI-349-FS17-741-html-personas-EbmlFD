Pernasia Platt

Retired

"Life's more than just a party, it's my party!"

About:

Meet Pernasia (Pronounced: "purn-asia" - like the continent). She is a 71 year old, wealthy female whose husband is no longer with us. She currently lives in a mansion in Beverly Hills by herself, but has many wild house parties. She is retired from being the owner 12 nail salons (NailTacular) and is in love with the stars (and not just the Hollywood ones, but the real ones too).

Attitude:

And boy does she have some. She can be very whiney and quite the drama queen, but is willing to invest in the future. In this case, the future being Spacation. She only likes visiting websites that have a "Pinterest-type flair" to them.

Goals:
  1. To go to space with all the Hollywood stars.
  2. To bring the party to space and show people you're never too old to party.
  3. To live her life in a more exciting fashion than her late husband, Gerald, did.
Behavior:

Without her smartphone, Pernasia Platt would be lost. But she also tends to get lost with it too. She can never figure out how to use the maps app, but is fluent when it comes to anything Instagram or Pinterest. "It's just the simple design." she said one time when asked why she likes Instagram over Facebook. She is immediately drawn in by websites and advertisements with artistic layouts. She once clicked on a phishing ad and lost $400 because the pop-up was so pretty, she just had to believe it was real.
