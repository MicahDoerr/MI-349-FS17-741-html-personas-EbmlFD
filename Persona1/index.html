Quantavious Permuddle

CEO of Spacation

"The world is not the limit, the edges of space are."

About:

Quantavious is a 34 year old imaginary. He is from Detroit, Michigan and is the brain that designed the idea of Spacation.

Attitude:

Permuddle is motivated by his love for exotic vacations. This is what turns his wheels, keeps the flame burning, floats his banana.

Goals:
  1. To be the first to create vacations in space a reality
  2. To create a friendly and fun work environment that makes every employee want to come in every day.
  3. To eat the most slices of cheesecake in 1 min and take the world record away from his weird cousin Tom.
Behavior:

Quantavious Permuddle is hasty. He once was using Amazon and ordered 236 cheescakes without thinking. He loves his smartphone. If a website is created for Spacation, he wants it made mobile-friendly. He is often frustrated at others lack of taste in clothing. Clothing is key for Permuddle. He is easy to please and has a dry sense of humor, but always thinks he is hilarious.
