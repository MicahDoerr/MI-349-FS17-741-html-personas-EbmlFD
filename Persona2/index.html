John Vanderbilt

Executive Director of NASA Relations

"It needs to be done faster."

About:

John is a 56 year old male originally from Alabama, but now resides in Austin, Texas. He is the executive director of NASA relations and is proud of his job and his family heritage. John is extremely goal-driven and needs things to be done his way.

Attitude:

Vanderbilt is motivated by two things: honor and churros. He decied to partner with Spacation because in his past he dreamt of vacationing in space and the CEO, Quantiavious Permuddle, offered him a lifetime supply of Churros and the first seat on the first ship to depart.

Goals:
  1. To beat the Russians to vacation opportunities in space.
  2. To be world-reknowned and on the cover of Forbes magazine.
  3. Desires to one day have a persnal Mexican chef to make him homemade churros.
Behavior:

When it comes to getting things done with John Vanderbilt, nothing can ever be done fast enough. He is very easily angered and is known as "Mr. Baked Potato Head" - and this is not just because of the appearance of his sunburnt bald head. He is known to strut around his office at NASA and just look over his employee's shoulders, just to make them nervous. He refuses to get a Facebook and will only used LinkedIn for any type of social media. When visiting a website, he is frustrated by unkempt designs and unfamiliar layouts. John likes things to be simple and easy-to-use - no extra flair.
